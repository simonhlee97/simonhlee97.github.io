A little bit about me...

I grew up in Indianapolis and have lived most of my life here. Not surprisingly, I love basketball, but never got into auto racing. After studying at Indiana University, I taught English in Korea for a few years. I went to DePaul University for grad school and taught math in Chicago. I enjoyed teaching, but wanted to pursue my interest in tech and try a new career. August 2017, I took my web-dev interests to the next level by signing up for the Accelerated JavaScript program at Eleven Fifty Academy. I enjoy problem-solving and building full-stack applications. I am open-minded and flexible, so I enjoy working alone and working with a team. Outside of coding, I love books, tennis, dogs, and photography.
