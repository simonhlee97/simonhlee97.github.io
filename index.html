I grew up in Indianapolis and lived most of my life here. Not surprisingly, I love basketball, but never got into NASCAR or auto racing. After studying Business at Indiana University, I taught English in Korea for a few years. I went to DePaul for grad school and taught math in Chicago. I enjoyed teaching, but wanted to pursue my interest in tech and try a new career. August 2017, I decided to take my web-dev interests to the next level by signing up for the full-stack JavaScript program at Eleven Fifty Academy. The 12-week coding bootcamp requires students to complete some projects, including this portfolio. I enjoy problem-solving and creating technology that can help people. I love working with data and databases, so I am looking forward to building apps using MongoDB and PostgreSQL. I am open-minded and flexible, so I enjoy working alone and working with a team. Outside of coding, I love books, tennis, dogs, and photography.
